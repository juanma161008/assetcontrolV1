# Information Security Objectives

## Objectives and Metrics
| Objective | Metric | Target | Owner | Review Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Reduce high/critical vulnerabilities | Time to remediate | <= 14 days | Engineering Lead | Monthly |
| Ensure backup reliability | Successful backup jobs | 100% weekly | Operations | Weekly |
| Maintain access governance | Access reviews completed | 100% quarterly | Security Lead | Quarterly |
| Improve incident response | Triage time | <= 4 hours | Security Lead | Quarterly |
| Maintain awareness | Staff trained | 100% annually | Security Lead | Annual |

## Evidence
- Scan reports, backup logs, access review logs, incident tickets, training records.
