# Incident Response Plan

## Objectives
- Contain, eradicate, and recover from security incidents.
- Preserve evidence and maintain communication.

## Severity Levels
- Low: no data impact, limited scope.
- Medium: service degradation or limited data exposure.
- High: confirmed breach or significant outage.

## Steps
1. Identify and classify incident.
2. Contain affected systems.
3. Eradicate root cause.
4. Recover service and validate.
5. Post-incident review and lessons learned.

## Communication
- Security Lead informs stakeholders.
- Legal/compliance notified when required.
- Customer notification guided by regulation.

## Evidence
- Incident tickets and timelines.
- Forensic artifacts and logs.
