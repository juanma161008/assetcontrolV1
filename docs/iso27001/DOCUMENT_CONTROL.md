# Document Control

## Purpose
Ensure ISMS documented information is approved, updated, and traceable.

## Rules
- Each document has an owner and last update date.
- Changes require review and approval by the document owner.
- Archived versions are retained for at least 12 months.

## Storage
- Primary location: `docs/iso27001/`.
- Evidence folder: `docs/iso27001/evidence/`.

## Review Cycle
- Policy documents: annual.
- Operational procedures: annual or after material changes.
