# ISMS Policy

## Purpose
Define the commitment of MicroCinco to protect information assets in AssetControl and maintain an effective ISMS aligned with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

## Policy Statement
MicroCinco is committed to:
- Preserve confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.
- Meet legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements.
- Continuously improve the ISMS through risk management and measurable objectives.

## Objectives
- Prevent unauthorized access to AssetControl data.
- Enforce strong credential handling and authentication controls.
- Ensure service availability meets agreed SLAs.
- Detect and respond to security incidents in a timely manner.
- Maintain audit trails for critical actions.

## Governance
- ISMS Owner: Security Lead (assign name).
- Data Owner: Product Owner (assign name).
- Technical Owner: Engineering Lead (assign name).

## Compliance
All personnel and suppliers with access to AssetControl must follow this policy.
Exceptions require documented approval and risk acceptance.

## Review
This policy is reviewed at least annually or after significant changes.
