# Business Continuity Plan

## Objectives
- Maintain essential service during disruptions.
- Define recovery priorities.

## Critical Services
- Authentication and access control.
- Asset registry and maintenance workflows.
- Audit logging.

## Strategy
- Redundant hosting or rapid redeploy capability.
- Tested backups and restore procedures.
- Runbook for outage response.

## Testing
- Annual tabletop exercise.
- Post-test corrective actions.
