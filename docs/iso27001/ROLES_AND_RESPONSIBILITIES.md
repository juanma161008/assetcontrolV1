# Roles and Responsibilities

## Core Roles
- Security Lead: Owns the ISMS, risk management, audits, and continuous improvement.
- Engineering Lead: Implements technical controls and secure SDLC.
- Product Owner: Approves scope, requirements, and risk acceptance.
- Operations: Manages environments, backups, monitoring, and incident response.
- Support: Handles user requests and follows access control policy.

## Responsibility Matrix (RACI)
- Policy updates: Security Lead (A/R), Engineering (C), Product (C)
- Risk assessment: Security Lead (A/R), Engineering (C), Product (C)
- Change management: Engineering Lead (A/R), Operations (R), Security (C)
- Incident response: Security Lead (A), Operations (R), Engineering (C), Support (C)
- Access provisioning: Operations (R), Security (C), Product (A)

## Training
All staff must complete security awareness training at least annually.
Records are tracked in `docs/iso27001/TRAINING_AWARENESS.md`.
